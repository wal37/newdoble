Privacy Policy

How Dobleu handles participant, inquiry, and study-related information.

This policy explains what information we collect from visitors, prospective customers, and research participants, and how it may be used in relation to the Dobleu platform.

Information we collect

We may collect names, contact details, organization details, participant profile information, eligibility responses, communications, and basic website interaction data when users contact us, request access, or join a study pipeline.

How we use it

We use information to respond to inquiries, manage access requests, support participant workflows, improve platform operations, communicate study-related updates, and maintain administrative records.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Limited information may be shared with service providers or authorized research partners when needed to operate the platform, support a study workflow, or deliver contracted services.

Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to support participant operations, customer relationships, legal obligations, security review, and service administration.

Your choices

You can request updates, corrections, or removal of your contact details from newsletters and non-essential outreach by contacting us directly.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact contact@Dobleu.net.
